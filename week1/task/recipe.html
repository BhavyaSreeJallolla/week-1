Classic Chicken Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 lb (500g) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • 2 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 4 green cardamom pods
  • 4 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt, to taste

procedure:

  1. Marinate Chicken: In a bowl, combine chicken pieces, yogurt, half of the minced garlic and ginger, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and let it marinate for at least 1 hour or overnight in the refrigerator.
  2. Cook Rice: Rinse basmati rice in cold water until the water runs clear. In a large pot, bring water to a boil, add rice, and cook until it is 70% cooked (it should still be firm). Drain and set aside.
  3. Sauté Onions: Heat oil in a large skillet or pot over medium heat. Add thinly sliced onions and sauté until they turn golden brown. Remove half of the onions and set aside for garnishing.
  4. Cook Chicken: To the remaining onions in the skillet, add the marinated chicken, minced garlic, ginger, cumin seeds, fennel seeds, and sauté for 5-7 minutes. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until the tomatoes soften and oil starts to separate.
  5. Layer Biryani: In a large heavy-bottomed pot, layer half of the par-cooked rice. Add the cooked chicken mixture on top. Layer with the remaining rice. Sprinkle fresh cilantro, mint leaves, and fried onions over the top. Drizzle with ghee and place lemon slices on top.
  6. Steam (Dum) Cook: Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid. Cook on low heat for about 20-25 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld and the rice to cook completely. Alternatively, you can seal the pot with aluminum foil before placing the lid to ensure it is tightly sealed.
  7. Serve: The yummy BIRYSNI is now read
